Flow Text

One common flow we've seen in many frameworks is a lack of support for truly responsive texts. While elements on the page resize fluidly, text still resizes on a fixed basis. To ameliorate this problem, for text heavy pages, we've created a class that fluidly scales text size and line-height to optimize readibility for the user. Line length stays between 45-80 characters and line height scales to larger on smaller screen. To see flow text in action, slowly resize your browser and watch the size of thi text body change!
